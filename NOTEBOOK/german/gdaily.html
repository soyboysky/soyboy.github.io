GERMAN DAILY NOTES

FEB 4 2019


  • Wenn = when, whenever, if
    • mostly present tense, future tense ("Whenever there is a snow day, I sleep")
  • all = when, back when, as
    • Mostly past tense ("when there was a show day, I slept")
  • starting a sentence with a Subordinating conjunction will divide a sentence into two clauses
    • "----- verb, verb -----"
    • If the first clause has more than one verb = "----- verb verb, verb ----- verb"
    • If 2 verbs then 1 verb or vice versa, Use the sentence structure relating to the amount of verbs in the different clauses
  • If the conjunction is in the middle = " ----- verb -----, sub conj. ----- verb" ("I got wet, because it rained")
    • If two, " ----- verb -----verb, sub conj. ----- verb verb"
