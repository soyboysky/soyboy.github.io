NIGHT ROUTINE





GENERAL ROUTINE

Like for the mornings, I often have little time at night to wind-down if i want to get a proper amount of sleep on school nights. Because of this, i often skip basic self-care entirely and just collapse into bed. Hopefully by having this routine, I will follow it closer, especially during school nights.

The following table is a general guideline of the ideal night routine, during school nights or nights of heavy studying.



Before i sleep, or study, set a time i want to complete everything and start winding down by. I will not have a set time, as i want flexibility.

30 minutes to Water and Tea - refrain from electronics and bright light, and prepare a glass of water and some chamomile tea.
25 minutes to Skincare - a shortened skincare routine for the night geos as follows; remember to wash face with lukewarm/warm water. The order of nightly skincare (01.10.19) is as follows -
  1. (ed)Burt's Bees gentle foaming cleanser
  2. (ed)Thayer's Witch Hazel Rose Astringent
  3. Murad brightening serum
  4. (ed)Burt's Bees sensitive night cream
  5. Brushing teeth
15 minutes to Journal - Write down a brief summary, even just bullet points, of your day. I need to work on this part.
10 minutes to Read - Set alarm for the morning, and open a leisure book and read a chapter or two.
